### 概念

#### IDC (Internet Data Center)

- 物理机房
- 网络
- 电力

示例：电信运营商

![the-planet-data-center-messy-640x426](http://git.caimi-inc.com/trinity/discussions/uploads/589251bd6139724a185a961d500faa3c/the-planet-data-center-messy-640x426.jpg)


#### IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service)

- 虚拟化
- 计算资源
- 存储资源

示例：AWS EC2、阿里云 ECS

可定制度很高，维护成本也很高

![image](http://git.caimi-inc.com/trinity/discussions/uploads/bd02279a4a00c0499499a84803025962/image.png)

#### PaaS (Platform as a Service)

- 操作系统
- 中间件
- 运行时服务

示例：AWS Elastic Beanstalk、Google App Engine

![image](http://git.caimi-inc.com/trinity/discussions/uploads/e1ad095d831fe6ebba67a39291609958/image.png)

#### SaaS (Software as a Service)

- 完整的软件服务解决方案
- 基本不需要什么专业知识

开箱即用

![image](http://git.caimi-inc.com/trinity/discussions/uploads/f97b642c526a5eabf2402325cbb0bfab/image.png)


#### Serverless / FaaS (Function as a Service)

- 基于事件驱动
- 只需编写对应事件处理器，几乎无需关注 backend

示例：AWS Lambda、阿里云函数计算

![product-page-diagram_Lambda-HowItWorks.68a0bcacfcf46fccf04b97f16b686ea44494303f](http://git.caimi-inc.com/trinity/discussions/uploads/4c297cd63c2eb708c807df27a7bd566e/product-page-diagram_Lambda-HowItWorks.68a0bcacfcf46fccf04b97f16b686ea44494303f.png)

![product-page-diagram_Lambda-WebApplications_2.c7f8cf38e12cb1daae9965ca048e10d676094dc1](http://git.caimi-inc.com/trinity/discussions/uploads/9355db71df4e1c9d437f4b92e145d303/product-page-diagram_Lambda-WebApplications_2.c7f8cf38e12cb1daae9965ca048e10d676094dc1.png)

### 总体趋势

IDC -> IAAS -> PAAS -> Serverless/FaaS

### 趋势特点

- 去中心化
- 去服务化
- 按需使用
- 开发维护成本更低
